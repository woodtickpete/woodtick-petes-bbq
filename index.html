Our Story

From the 1990s to Today

Woodtick Pete started making BBQ sauce back in the 1990s because nothing on the shelf had the bold, smoky flavor he wanted.

Every batch is proudly handcrafted in Cedar Falls, Iowa using real honey and quality ingredients. As a veteran-owned business, our mission is simple—make amazing BBQ sauce that families will enjoy.
